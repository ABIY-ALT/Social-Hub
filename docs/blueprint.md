# **App Name**: Social Central

## Core Features:

- Social Account Integration: Connect and manage multiple social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube).
- Content Scheduling: Plan and schedule social media posts using a drag-and-drop calendar with monthly and weekly views.
- AI Content Creation: Generate social media content based on prompts, content goals (awareness, promotion, education), and platform selection.
- AI Content Risk Check: Analyze social media content for potential risks (reputation, legal, security) and provide safe rewrite recommendations with this AI tool.
- Unified Inbox: Aggregate messages, comments, and replies from all connected social media accounts into a single feed with filters and actions.
- Analytics and Reporting: Track engagement, follower growth, and best-performing posts across platforms, with date range filters and export options.
- Team Collaboration: Manage team members, roles, approval workflows, and feedback within the platform.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep amber (#FF7E00) for trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to ensure a clean, uncluttered interface.
- Accent color: Vibrant orange (#FF7043) for CTAs and alerts to draw attention.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for a modern and neutral appearance that supports readability in both headlines and body text.
- Use minimalist and consistent icons for navigation and actions.
- Card-based grid layout for dashboards and data summaries.
- Subtle transitions and animations for loading states and user interactions.
